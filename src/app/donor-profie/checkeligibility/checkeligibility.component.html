Can you donate blood now?


These questions will help you check you are able to give blood.


  • Please run through them before you go along to your donation appointment - it will only take a minute or two.


  • They cover the most common reasons that stop people donating when they go along to give blood.


  • It could save you time or even a wasted journey. If you find you're unable to donate please remember to re-arrange any affected appointments.


  • Remember that our eligibility criteria is there to ensure that it is safe for you to donate and that it is safe for patients to receive your donations.


  • Your responses to these questions are anonymous and will not be saved in your donor record.


  • Any timings we mention are from the date of your appointment, not from today's date.
